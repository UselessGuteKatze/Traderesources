This section is intended to view information about tenders (auctions, tenders and tenders) for the provision of natural and national resources for use, as well as to familiarize yourself with the terms of tenders and their results published in the trading system through the web portal of the register of state property www.gosreestr.kz .

Access to the databases on tenders for granting the right to natural resources is provided to the seller-competent authority (employees of the relevant structural unit of the ministry or akimat), if employees have an electronic digital signature issued National Certification Authority of Kazakhstan.

Access to databases is provided on the basis of a written application (download the application form) of the competent authority submitted to the Information and Accounting Center joint-stock company, which is determined by the single operator in the field of state property accounting in accordance with Resolution of the Government of the Republic of Kazakhstan dated July 15, 2011 No. 802.
